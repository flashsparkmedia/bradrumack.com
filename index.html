Projects

Hawkins.png was built for a client of mine who runs a photography editing business. He needed a way to streamline his process of onboarding new clients and providing editing services through project assignments to his staff of editors. For him I created an SPA that handles file uploads, downloads and permissions management.

Clients are taken through the project creation process by picking packages options and then are prompted to upload their files. A project can then be assigned to an editor, who can download the files, make their edits, and reupload for approval. Upon approval, a notification is sent to the client that they can now download the edited files.

ToodFrucks is an SPA with the goal of promoting the food truck industry. Its objective is to give food truck owners the ability to connect with consumers. Through the app, food trucks are able to share their present location and hours, as well as future locations. Consumers are able to locate trucks in their area, and search using keywords or cusine types.

ToodFrucks is in continuous development. Due to the current world conditions, an official launch isn't yet planned, but a demo is available online. You'll find links below that log you in as either a consumer or a merchant. Long-term plans include online ordering, and possibly integration with food delivery services such as Doordash and Postmates.
